Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2025

Gastro Ledger ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance and expense tracking application designed to run entirely on your device. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting it.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal, financial, or usage data. All information entered into Gastro Ledger remains local to your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

2. Offline-Only Functionality

Gastro Ledger operates fully offline. The app does not require an internet connection, and it does not connect to any external services or networks.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your financial records and preferences are saved directly on your device. Because we do not have access to your data, you are responsible for securing your device and backing up your information as needed.

4. No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising platforms, or cloud services. Your data stays 100% private and offline.

5. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or process any personal information from children.
